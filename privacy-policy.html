Information We Collect

Direct interactions

  • Contact forms, WhatsApp messages, phone calls and event registrations
  • Identification data such as full name, email, phone number, nationality
  • Transaction data including preferred budgets, project interest, meeting notes

Automated data

  • Device, browser, and analytic signals captured via consented cookies
  • Campaign performance metrics and attribution identifiers
  • Site usage insights that are anonymised for product optimisation

Use of Information

Delivering Concierge-Level Service

Your data powers tailored property recommendations, curated reports, launch invitations, and investor relationship management. We may also use it to comply with KYC/AML requirements mandated by UAE law.

Legal Grounds

Consent & Legitimate Interest

We process personal data under explicit consent, contractual necessity when onboarding clients, or legitimate interest to provide premium advisory services. Marketing communications are always opt-in with instant opt-out links.

Data Sharing & Retention

Trusted Partners

Developers, banks, or conveyancing teams receive your data only after you authorise introductions.

Infrastructure

Data is hosted on encrypted servers within the GCC or EU, with role-based access and MFA protection.

Retention

Records are retained for seven years or the period required by UAE regulators, then securely deleted.

Your Privacy Rights

You can exercise the following rights at any time by emailing info@mdre.ae.

  • • Request access to the personal data we hold
  • • Ask for corrections or updates to inaccurate details
  • • Request deletion where legally permissible
  • • Object to processing or withdraw consent
  • • Request a portable copy of your data file

Escalation

Dubai Digital Authority

If we fail to satisfy a request, you may contact the UAE Data Office or the Dubai Digital Authority with your case ID and correspondence history.
